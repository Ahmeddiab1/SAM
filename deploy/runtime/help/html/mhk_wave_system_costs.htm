System Costs

SAM's marine and hyrdokinetic models for wave and tidal power use a fixed-charge-rate (FCR) method to calculate the levelized cost of energy from inputs on the Financial Parameters page. The FCR method requires a total installed cost and annual operating cost value, which you can calculate on the System Costs page.

The total installed cost is the sum of the total device cost, total balance of system cost, and total financial cost.

For each cost category, you can choose whether to define the cost by hand (user input) or to use a modeled value. When you choose to use the modeled value, SAM calculates the cost based on the inputs on the Wave or Tidal Energy Converter and Array pages.

Use modeled value

Choose this option for SAM to automatically calculate the cost.

Enter in $

Type a dollar value for the cost.

Enter in in $/kW

Type a cost per unit of rated capacity. The rated capacity is displayed under Total Installed Costs at the bottom of the System Costs page, and is defined on the Array page.

For information about a cost category, click the Information button next to the category's label.
